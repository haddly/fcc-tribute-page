My father Derek

Advice: Be positive and watch the road

Myself and my father at Brightlingsea in 2010. A place we spent so much of our childhood. 9 miles from Colchester.

  • Born in 1935 in Swansea, Wales
  • Remembers being woken during the night to go down to the air raid shelters during the war.
  • Was sent to a home shortly after the war with one of his brothers. They hated it and ran away
  • Loved to read from a young age. I think he read everything out there
  • Loved poetry. He had Dylan Thomas on vinyl.
  • loved to write. He kept a journal for many decades
  • While engaged at 20, he decides to join the Merchant Navy to get out of marriage and to see the world
  • He writes to his fianc√© once he has shipped off to let her know he can't get married
  • Sails the world
  • Gets malaria in Africa, and thinks he's gonna die
  • Survives malaria but sleeps with a prostitute and thinks he may have AIDS
  • Doesn't get AIDS
  • Spends a few years as a Gastarbeiter in Germany working on building sites
  • Applies to college at the age of 30
  • Gets accepted and spends 3 years at Oxford University
  • Meets a German guy by the name of Hans, a friend he knows until the end of his life
  • Meets his first wife Joelle in Oxford and has two children, Luc and Rachel
  • Later divorces and meets his next partner with whom he has four children. One of them me
  • Teaches guitar at the University of Essex while doing a degree in PPE
  • Spends many summer days at Brightlingsea with us
  • Spends his retirement years reading, writing, traveling, and being positive
  • 2010 is diagnosed with dementia
  • Stays positive and keeps fighting for nearly 6 years. A tough time for the family but mostly for our mum
  • April 2016 dies peacefully aged 80
